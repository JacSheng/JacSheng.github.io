Course Overview

Do you aspire to own your own art studio one day?

The Diploma in Fine Art offers opportunities for the students to develop creative skills and visual language to probe reality and express through the tangibility of form. This course stresses on critical and creative thinking along with strong studio-based studies.


Students with drawing, painting, printmaking, figure drawing and non-traditional experimental projects which expose the students to the visual language necessary for them to explore and discover their individual artistic inclination. Advanced and in-depth studies will be implemented progressively to enable our students to develop skills and techniques. At the same time, students will learn to express their ideas, perceptions, imaginations, responses and creativity.
