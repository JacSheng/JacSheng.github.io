Course Overview

Would you like to transform your spaces?

The Interior Design course aims to equip students with the ability to create both the aesthetic and technical aspects required for professional interior design practice.


Throughout the program, students will learn to develop creative and innovative design abilities by working on individual projects which deal with the theoretical, conceptual and practical aspects of interior design. Students will be exposed to complex problem of modern environments in the context of style, taste, culture and humanity. We maintain a close relationship with the industrial sectors in the country and keep our faculty and students updated with current developments in design and technology. At the end of the program, students will be able to meet the challenges and demands of the design profession in an effective manner.
