HOW TO COOK CHICKEN CURRY

Ingredients:

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves

Step:

  1. Saute curry paste in hot oil.
  2. Add in chicken
  3. Continue to saute until chicken release oil
  4. Add in water,temarind paste,curry leaves,potato,onion,sugar and salt.
  5. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked throughly
  6. Add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate
  7. Remove and serve
